Marketing specialists help develop, execute, and monitor marketing programs across a variety of channels. Their work includes researching the market, analyzing trends to help define the organization's marketing strategy, and providing advice as to how to best reach the target market. Depending on the role, marketing specialists may also help with the coordination of events such as trade shows or conferences.

Pakar pemasaran membantu mengembangkan, melaksanakan, dan memantau program pemasaran di berbagai saluran. Pekerjaan mereka termasuk meneliti pasar, menganalisis tren untuk membantu menentukan strategi pemasaran organisasi, dan memberikan saran tentang cara terbaik mencapai pasar sasaran. Bergantung pada perannya, spesialis pemasaran juga dapat membantu mengoordinasikan acara seperti pameran dagang atau konferensi.
